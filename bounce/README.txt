BOUNCE (BREAKOUT)

by Andrew Oakes

a basic arcade game built using the JIG framework for Slick2D


IF YOU ARE NOT GRADING THIS ASSIGNMENT COVER THIS SECTION UP WITH YOUR HAND OR SOMETHING
****************************************************************************************
Cheat codes: TAB to go to next level, backspace to go back a level
             up arrow for more lives, f to destroy all bricks
-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_

The controls are simple enough

"A"     -   To move the paddle left.
"D"     -   To move the paddle right.
"Enter" -   To launch the ball TOWARDS YOUR MOUSE POINTER.

The objective is also simple, destroy all the bricks to win the level.
After ten bounces off of the paddle, the ball will stick to your paddle again.
Each time the ball bounces off the paddle it speeds up a bit, but it is reset upon relaunch.
Also, each bounce adds a score multiplier for the bricks destroyed during that bounce, max of 10.
